# Accounts

LocalPaloma is the Paloma testnet that comes preconfigured with opinionated, sensible defaults for standard testing environments, including one validator and ten accounts with GRAIN balances.

LocalPaloma has the following advantages over a public testnet:

  • Easily modifiable world states
  • Quick to reset for rapid iterations
  • Simple simulations of different scenarios
  • Controllable validator behavior
